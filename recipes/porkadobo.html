PORK ADOBO

Description

This is a classic Filipino dish that is savory and slightly tangy, perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs pork belly, cut into cubes
  • 1 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 cup vinegar
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 5 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 teaspoon black peppercorns
  • 1 tablespoon sugar (optional)
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. In a large pot, combine pork, soy sauce, vinegar, water, garlic, bay leaves, and peppercorns.
  2. Bring to a boil over medium heat, then reduce to low heat and simmer for about 40 minutes or until the pork is tender.
  3. If desired, add sugar to balance the flavors and adjust salt to taste.
  4. Serve hot with steamed rice.
