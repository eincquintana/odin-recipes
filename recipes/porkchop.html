PORK CHOP

Description

This is a simple and delicious pork chop recipe that is perfect for any meal.

Ingredients

  • 2-4 pork chops
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 tablespoon fresh herbs (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. Season the pork chops with salt, pepper, garlic powder, and paprika.
  3. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Sear the pork chops for 3-4 minutes on each side until golden brown.
  5. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for 15-20 minutes until cooked through.
  6. Let the pork chops rest for a few minutes before serving.
